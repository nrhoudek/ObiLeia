Katie

Meet Katie! Katie is 25 years old and graduated from The University of Arizona in 2020. She majored in Biochemistry and has two Associates of Science Degrees: Associates of Clinical Research and an Associates of Science. Katie has always had a creative itch that needs to be scratched. From drawing and painting to interior designing, Katie has already had an interest and appreciation for art. Recently, she has stepped into the world of photography, and with her camera in hand, she is ready to shoot something. While she is learning the ropes to photography, she has been using her cats as practice since they are so photogenic, but also mainly because they are, well, close. Outside of cat photos, Katie loves to hike, and her new-found hobby, fortunately, pairs up perfectly. Between nature, and the ecosystem of a small apartment, Katie has plenty of opportunity to take those shots that makes something like this site possible.
